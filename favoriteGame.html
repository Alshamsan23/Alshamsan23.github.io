Favorite Game

It is a team tactics first person shooter (FPS) game, this game is a multiplayer game in which you can enjoy with your friends. The game consists of 36 different maps where you can play 4 unique gamemodes. Personally, I got to know this game from my cousin, he taught me the basics of the game and introduced me to the world of Overwatch. Briefly after I was introduced to the game I managed to improve my in game skills and finally reach the top 500 charts which is the highest possible rank to gain in Overwatch 2.
